What You Need To Know

How long do I have to file a personal injury claim?

In California, most personal injury claims must be filed within two years from the date of injury, subject to specific exceptions for minors, incapacity, or absent defendants.

Do I pay anything upfront?

No. Omega Law Group works on a contingency basis, taking its fee from the recovery; if the firm does not win, you pay nothing out of pocket.

Should I talk to the insurance company first?

The firm advises against speaking to any insurance company before you understand your rights, because insurers are focused on minimizing payout, not maximizing your recovery.

Will my case go to trial?

Many personal injury cases resolve through negotiation, but Omega Law Group prepares each matter as if it will be tried to ensure leverage if a fair settlement is not offered.
